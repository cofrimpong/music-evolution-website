Challenges with Streaming

Streaming services have revolutionized the way we consume music, offering instant access to millions of tracks. However, the financial model of streaming platforms presents significant challenges for artists. Unlike the era of physical album sales, streaming pays artists based on a per-stream basis, which often results in much lower income.

Many artists find it difficult to make a sustainable income through streaming alone. The payout per stream is small, and the revenue distribution heavily favors larger artists with millions of streams, leaving independent and smaller artists struggling. Moreover, the complexities of royalty calculations make it challenging for artists to understand how much they are earning and how they can maximize their income.
