Possible Solutions for the Music Industry

To ensure a more equitable music industry, several changes need to be implemented. One of the most crucial steps is revising the royalty payout models used by streaming platforms. Increasing the per-stream payout or introducing a user-centric payout model could help ensure that artists receive fair compensation for their work.

Additionally, increased transparency in how royalties are calculated and distributed can help artists understand their earnings better and foster trust in the system. Supporting independent artists through grants, promotional opportunities, and fair contracts can also empower smaller artists to thrive in the industry. Collaborations between artists and new platforms that prioritize fairness and artist control may also play a crucial role in building a more sustainable music ecosystem.
